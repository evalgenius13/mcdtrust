The Tool of the Ultra-Wealthy

The ultra-wealthy use a powerful legal structure called a **dynasty trust** to grow their fortunes tax-efficiently for generations. This is how their wealth compounds for centuries.

⏳

Lasts for Generations

Can last 100+ years, automatically passing wealth to children and grandchildren without extra legal costs or taxes each time.

🛡️

Protects Assets

The money in the trust is legally separate from your family, protecting it from things like lawsuits, business failures, or divorces.

📈

Unlocks Compound Growth

By avoiding taxes and fees each generation, the money can grow much faster, creating true generational wealth over time.

The Catch: Right now, these trusts are only available to the super-rich because they require over a million dollars and tens of thousands in legal fees to set up.

A Solution: Democratize Dynasty Trusts

This model makes the ultra-wealthy's most powerful wealth-building instrument accessible, simple, and safe for working families.

🏦

Trusted Financial Partners

Major, well-known financial companies would hold the money in secure accounts, investing it in simple, proven index funds and providing regular reports.

⚖️

Independent Oversight

A nonprofit organization would act as a co-trustee, like a watchdog, to make sure the financial partners are doing their job and that every family's interests are protected for the full 100 years.

🔗

Transparent Public Records

Key information about the trusts would be published on a public ledger, creating a permanent and tamper-proof record that anyone can check for themselves.
