What is a Dynasty Trust?

Dynasty trusts are the proven legal structures that established wealthy families have used for centuries to preserve and transfer wealth across generations.

🏛️

Multi-Generational Duration

Lasts 100+ years, automatically transferring wealth to children, grandchildren, and future descendants without requiring new legal structures.

🛡️

Asset Protection

Assets are legally protected from creditors, divorces, and lawsuits that could affect beneficiaries, preserving wealth for intended purposes.

💰

Tax Efficiency

Bypasses estate taxes at each generation, keeping more wealth in your family instead of losing 40% to taxes each time wealth transfers.
